About Aroma Dew

Natural Ingredients

Every Aroma Dew soap is built on a base of cold-pressed coconut oil, castor oil, and shea butter — chosen for their skin-nourishing and lathering properties. Botanical extracts, clays, and essential oils do the rest.

FAQs

What is in Aroma Dew soaps?
Our soaps are made with cold-pressed coconut oil, shea butter, castor oil, and pure essential oils. We never use synthetic fragrances, sulphates, parabens, or artificial colours.
Are they safe for sensitive skin?
Yes. All five variants are formulated without harsh surfactants. If you have very sensitive skin, we recommend our Lavender Calm bar which uses the gentlest botanical blend.
How long does one bar last?
Each 100g bar lasts approximately 4–6 weeks with daily use. Using a well-draining soap dish between uses significantly extends its life.
How are Aroma Dew soaps made?
We use the cold-process method — oils and lye water are combined at low temperatures to preserve the integrity of every botanical ingredient. Each batch is poured into moulds, cut by hand, and cured for 6 weeks before sale.
